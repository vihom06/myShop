About Our Store.

Welcome to Vikram Kirana and General Store, your trusted destination for daily essentials and household goods in Vasant Nagri, Vasai East. We take pride in serving our local community with a wide range of quality products, including groceries, pulses, grains, snacks, personal care items, and general household necessities. Our store is known for cleanliness, friendly service, and honest pricing. Whether you're shopping for your family's needs or picking up a few quick items, we ensure a smooth and pleasant experience.
